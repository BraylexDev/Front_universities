The Academic Ranking of Arab Universities is an annual publication that evaluates and ranks higher education institutions within the Arab region. It considers various factors such as research output, quality of faculty, international collaboration, and student satisfaction to provide a comprehensive overview of the performance and excellence of Arab universities. This ranking helps to identify top-performing institutions and encourages healthy competition among them, ultimately contributing to the development of the Arab educational landscape.
